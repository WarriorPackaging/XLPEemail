OP-30 Open Cell XLPE

The OP-30 is a 1.9lb density, open-cell chemically crosslinked PE that is engineered to provide great sound and moisture absorption, excellent chemical resistance, lightweight, and yet, strong and durable typical properties. OP-30 does not release poisonous gas or deteriorate when exposed to chemicals. In recent years, it is widely used in medical packaging for instruments where low-temperature sterilization is required. Other common applications include:

  • Oil & chemical absorption due to its hydrophilic characteristic
  • Medical packaging when low-temperature sterilization is required
  • Sound absorption for automotive, housing, and home appliances
  • Gasketing in electronic devices
  • General packaging
